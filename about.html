About Us

What is Spark2Code?

Spark2Code aims to inspire middle school students into the field of computer science. Since middle school does not offer computer science in the academic curriculum (in the SDUHSD district), our goal is to have the students exposed to an environment filled with technology by introducing them to code. For example, the students will learn to code their own unique website from scratch. Not only will this club get the student into computer science, but it will also be an exciting experience where the student will be able to express their creativity through different web designs. The club is operated by passionate high school volunteers who are willing to teach the middle school students the computer science curriculum. The high school volunteers do not need to have any coding experience as they will be learning the material while teaching the middle school students.

Mission Statement:

"Our mission is to help younger students find the spark to explore their passion for computer science by providing easy, fun, and engaging computer science courses and curriculum to schools that do not provide any"
